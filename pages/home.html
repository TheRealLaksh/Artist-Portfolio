Meet the Artist - Shruti Misra

My dedication to acting and storytelling is the driving force behind my pursuits. Theatre, for me, transcends mere performance; it is a profound means of human connection. My experience spans a diverse array of roles, from the spirited Queen of Hearts to the composed and profound Gandhari. Each character has challenged me to evolve, explore various facets of the human condition, and continually refine my theatrical abilities.

Beyond performance, my ambition lies in leveraging the art of storytelling as a catalyst for positive change. Theatre possesses a unique power to broaden perspectives and foster new understandings, and I believe it can be an influential instrument for raising awareness about sustainability. This conviction fuels my aspiration to study abroad, where I can immerse myself in different cultures and integrate new viewpoints into my craft. My goal is not simply to perform, but to fully engage with theatre, using it to create significant impact and bring narratives of hope, resilience, and responsibility to a global audience.
