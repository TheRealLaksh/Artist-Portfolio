Iridescente - Alice in Wonderland

Role: Evil Red Heart Queen

Performance Highlights

Actor's Notes: Playing the Queen of Hearts pushed me to embrace extremes. I had to master rapid shifts in emotion while keeping my body language bold and exaggerated. One of my biggest challenges was controlling instinctive expressions of disgust and instead using deliberate eyebrow movements and sudden gestures to capture her larger-than-life personality. This role taught me that true acting practice happens off stage, where discipline and repetition build the confidence to be unapologetically expressive on stage.
